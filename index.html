About Me

Ahsanul Abeer (Ahsan) is a first-generation Bangladeshi-American student studying Business Administration and Management at the City College of New York. During his time at City College, Ahsan was involved in a number of prestigious programs including Stanford d. School’s University Innovation Fellows program and the Colin Powell School’s Edward I. Koch Fellowship in Public Service. He also participated in the leadership at City College as a Campus Representative and Senator of the Undergraduate Student Government, and as Secretary of the Muslim Student Organization.

Ahsan is also a self-taught web developer, a trained IT specialist and business analyst, and an experienced business development professional. With his combined expertise, Ahsan has had the pleasure of working in organizations of all shapes and sizes from startups to midsized firms to large organizations. His most recent experience is at the NYC Department of Education where he works as a Business & Website Analyst Intern.
